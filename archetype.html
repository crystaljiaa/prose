Archetype


Archetype appears in areas relating to behavior, modern psychological theory, and literary analysis. An archetype can be a statement, pattern of behavior, or prototype (model) which other statements, patterns of behavior, and objects copy or emulate, a constantly recurring symbol or motif in literature, painting, or mythology. In various seemingly unrelated cases in classic storytelling, media, etc., characters or ideas sharing similar traits recur.
